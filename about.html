About Budget Birdie

Budget Birdie is an advanced budget management tool developed by the Budget Birdie team. It implements tools such as client side storage, CSS frameworks, forms that accept and store user data and modal browser alerts.

Budget Birdie was developed by a team of four students at Rice University (kinda). It uses a mix of HTML, CSS and JavaScript to provide a quick, on the fly way to calculate, categorize and manipulate your expenses (by filtering, adding, deleting, etc) so that you are able to meet your financial goals and remain accountable to the most important person of all - YOURSELF!

Budget Birdie also contains redirectable navigation links to the three different parts of the website - the main page, the contact page (for complaints/requests), and this page, which provides additional information about the website!

We hope you enjoy your time with BudgetBirdie. Good luck!
